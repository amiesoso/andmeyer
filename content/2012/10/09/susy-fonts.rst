A Font Family for Susy
======================

The first drafts
of the "GreenMud Susy" fonts
are kerned, complete,
and roughly `in place`_.
Susy chapters now use her font,
JJ chapters use his,
and the remainder is split
until I flesh out a neutral "narrator" font.

.. _in place: http://greengreenmud.com/
